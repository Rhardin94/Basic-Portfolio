About Me

I am currently a student of Northwestern University's Coding Bootcamp and I am fascinated by all things technology!
While earning my Bachelor's degree from Illinois State Universiy I planned to study both Acting and Computer Science/Information technology.

As most people are interested in multiple subjects, I split my focus between my two passions: Acting and Technology; however, as the semesters progressed I had to chose between my passions due to time constraints.

I do not regret my decision to chose Acting first, as I always planned to continue my studies in a technologically inclined field at a later date.

Thanks to Northwestern University's Coding Bootcamp, I now have the chance to not only study in a technology haven but to combine my creative side that I cultivated while studying acting as well!
